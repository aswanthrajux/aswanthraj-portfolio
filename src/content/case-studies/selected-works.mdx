<SectionEyebrow>2023</SectionEyebrow>

## A year of shipping at scale.

2023 was the year the design practice grew up. FolderWall introduced a new paradigm for app organisation on Android, using situational context instead of category logic. Hub and Lockscreen reimagined the space between idle and active into an intelligent ambient layer.

The year-end reel captures what shipped: from contextual app recommendations to the first commerce surfaces designed for passive moments.

<SectionEyebrow>2024</SectionEyebrow>

## AI-native from start to finish.

2024 was defined by moving fully into AI-native product design. Travel Moments brought 3D AI maps and generative travel videos to the living room. Glance TV evolved from screensaver to conversational commerce platform. Bedtime Stories explored what AI-generated narrative content could look and feel like on an ambient screen.

The year-end reel is thirty seconds of product work asking what the next screen could be.
